ABOUT

WHO WE ARE 
​CSW Development was formed in 2004 to assist growing community service providers with the implementation of their real estate strategy. We pursue this goal by gaining an in-depth understanding of our client’s business model. From this, we are able to identify the real estate related metrics which are necessary to position our clients to achieve success.

Both as property owners and developers, our business practices are based in the belief that the long-term prosperity generated by our work and our partners’ investment comes from the value added to the community at large. That value is the important and needed service of providing well maintained, competitively priced and fairly administered rental property to people who need space for their business.

OUR HISTORY
The CSW team has over 90 years of combined experience in the real estate industry. We have successfully completed more than 100 real estate projects with a combined market value of over $700 million. Through our breadth of experience representing a wide array of successful companies, CSW has developed an efficient system supported by an experienced team to perpetuate the growth of our clients.
